Sunrise Public School

Nurturing brilliant minds, shaping purposeful futures. Our integrated academic framework encourages rigorous, discovery-based inquiry with a balanced pathway for co-scholastic excellence.

Our Vision Statement

To create an environment of holistic intellectual advancement where students learn to adapt seamlessly to global societal transitions while adhering strictly to high benchmarks of character, ethics, and innovation.

Our Core Mission

To deliver a robust scientific and humanitarian curriculum that promotes critical investigation, artistic exploration, digital competence, environmental stewardship, and empathetic communal responsibility.
